📂 Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

About the Course

The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) is a four-year degree program designed to prepare students like me for professional careers in various office and business environments. It builds strong foundations in modern office procedures, effective communication, records management, computer applications, and professional work ethics. As a 2nd-year student at the University of Antique, I am continuously developing the skills needed to become a reliable and efficient office professional.

What I Am Learning

  • Office Procedures and Workflow Management
  • Business and Professional Communication
  • Records and Information Management
  • Computer Applications (MS Office Suite, Google Workspace)
  • Work Ethics and Professionalism
  • Meeting and Event Coordination
  • Basic Accounting and Business Mathematics
  • Human Relations and Customer Service
  • Document Preparation and Business Correspondence

Why This Course Matters to Me

Office administration is the backbone of every successful organization. It ensures smooth daily operations, proper handling of confidential information, and organized workflows. By studying this course, I aim to become detail-oriented, responsible, and skilled enough to support businesses and help them run efficiently. I value order, accuracy, and good service — qualities that this program helps me strengthen every day.
